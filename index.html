Resistor Piano

This one uses a resistor ladder to help the Arduino understand what key is being pressed. Code then outputs a frequency for the piezo to play. I was inspire when I saw someone playing an electric piano. I thought to myself, "I could build that" so I did. If only I could play the piano.

Coolometer

Playing some tunes was fun but I wanted to learn how to print a message to a screen. Better yet let's try to understand a speedometer. Here is my attempt at building that. Execpt instead of counting the rpm and converting that to a speed I just use a potentiometer that controls a servo motor. Also I called it a coolometer because naming things isn't my job.
